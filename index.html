Welcome to Webtrotter

Introduction:

Have you ever wondered how the internet actually gives you the possibility to get in tuch with your friends on the other side of the world and how your computer knows where to find them? This introduction is for you to start being aware of your internettravel:

Webtrotter:

Is a program made in collaboration between a designer and a porgrammer, for you to start following you world wide web travel through various communication companies. The program will provide you visually with knowledge of:

How many communication companies one webtravel consist of where they are physically located and which communication companies you visit the most
